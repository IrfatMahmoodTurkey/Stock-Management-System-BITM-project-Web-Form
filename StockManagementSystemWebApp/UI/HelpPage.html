No you cannot login without user id and password.
Dashboard

Overview

After login into our website you will redirect to our software Dashboard . Under overview section you can see basic report of our software activity.

Category Setup

Under Category Setup , you can set your product category name by using name input box . And all category name will show serially in same page.

Company Setup

Under Company Setup , you can set your customer Company Name by using name input box . And all company name will show serially in same page. And more..

Item Setup

Under Item Setup , you can set your Items depending on Company Name & Category .

Stock in

Under Stock In section you can stock your items and quanity under a company name. Select company and items and quanity how much you want to stock in . Before do that you have to setup at least 1 items in Item Setup section .

Stock out

Under Stock Out section you can stock out your saved items. Just select Company and Item then quantity how much item you want to stock out. Then you can choose 3 options which is Sell, Damage and Lost. Before do that if you have to stock in your items in Stock In section .

Search & View Item Summery

Under Search & View Item Summery section you can view your all items under company and its category. Just select Company and category and click on search button. A complete items list will be show serially with items quanity .

View Sales between two dates

On this View Sales between two dates section you can check when you sold your items , Just simply select your from date and targeted date to view your sells between two date .
